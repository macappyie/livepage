What We Offer

Services built around how people actually learn to trade

Courses alone rarely change behaviour. That's why every Money Assure plan pairs structured lessons with mentorship, live practice and an active community.

Structured Courses

A 12-module curriculum covering everything from stock market basics to advanced options strategies, delivered online or offline.

1-on-1 Mentorship

Dedicated mentor sessions to review your watchlist, your trades and your risk management, tailored to your goals.

Live Market Sessions

Join live trading-hour sessions where your mentor walks through real charts and real decision-making in real time.

Portfolio Reviews

Periodic reviews of your holdings and allocation to check they still match your goals and risk appetite.

Community Access

A moderated WhatsApp and Telegram group for daily doubt-clearing, market notes and peer discussion.

Trading Psychology Coaching

Structured exercises to build discipline, manage fear and greed, and stick to your trading plan.

How It Works

From enrolment to your first trade plan

1
Enrol & onboard

Choose an online or offline batch and complete a short onboarding call to understand your goals.

2
Learn the modules

Work through the 12-module curriculum with live, mentor-led classes and plenty of doubt-clearing along the way.

3
Practice with a mentor

Apply concepts in paper trades and live sessions, with direct feedback from your mentor.

4
Build your own plan

Graduate with a documented trading and risk plan you can keep refining on your own.

Online Classes

Live, interactive sessions over video call, accessible from anywhere in India — every class is taught in real time.

Offline Classes

Small, in-person batches at our home setup in Taloja Phase 2, Navi Mumbai, with hands-on group practice and whiteboard chart walkthroughs.
