Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

Money Assure is a one-person stock market education service run by an individual educator, not a large company. This page explains, in plain terms, what information is collected from you and how it's used.

1. What Information Is Collected

When you fill out the contact form, message on WhatsApp or Telegram, or enrol in a course, the following may be collected:

  • Your name, phone number and email address
  • Whatever you write in the contact form or in chat — your questions, interests, course preference
  • Basic, automatic website data such as which pages you visited and what browser you used

2. Why This Information Is Collected

  • To reply to your questions and help you enrol in a course
  • To let you know about batch timings, schedule changes and course material
  • To understand which parts of the website are actually useful, so it can be improved

That's it — there is no sales team, no marketing department and no data broker involved.

3. How It's Kept Safe

Reasonable care is taken to keep your information safe — for example, not sharing it publicly and not storing more than is needed. That said, no one can promise 100% security over the internet, and this is not a claim of guaranteed protection.

4. Is Your Data Shared With Anyone?

Your information is never sold. It's only used directly by Money Assure to communicate with you. If a payment or messaging platform (such as WhatsApp, Telegram, or a payment gateway) is used to process your enrolment, your information passes through that platform as part of using it — the same way it would for any business using WhatsApp or UPI.

5. Cookies

The website may use basic cookies to remember simple preferences, like language choice. You're free to block or clear cookies from your browser settings at any time.

6. Your Choices

You can ask to see, correct, or delete the information held about you at any time — just message support@moneyassure.app or the WhatsApp number below. You can also opt out of any promotional message by simply replying "STOP" or telling the team directly.

7. Age Requirement

Money Assure's courses are meant for individuals aged 18 and above. Information is not knowingly collected from minors.

8. Changes to This Page

If anything about this policy changes, this page will be updated with a new date at the top.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Reach out directly at support@moneyassure.app or +91 93262 37560.
