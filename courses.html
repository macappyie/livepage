Course Mein Kya Sikhoge
MOD 01
Stock Market Basics

NSE & BSE, what a share is, and how to open and use a Demat account.

MOD 02
Fundamental Analysis

Reading P/E ratios, balance sheets and financial statements to judge a business.

MOD 03
Technical Analysis

Charts, candlesticks, and identifying support & resistance zones.

MOD 04
Sector Analysis

Comparing banking, IT, pharma and auto sectors to spot relative strength.

MOD 05
Intraday & Swing Trading

Entry and exit rules, stop-loss placement and short-term risk management.

MOD 06
Options Trading (F&O)

Calls, puts, and strategies such as straddles and iron condors.

MOD 07
Risk Management Strategies

Position hedging, diversification and disciplined capital allocation.

MOD 08
Market Psychology

Understanding fear & greed, and the behavioural biases that move markets.

MOD 09
Trading Psychology

Building discipline and patience, and learning from — not repeating — losses.

MOD 10
Position Sizing

How much capital to allocate per trade and how to calculate lot sizes.

MOD 11
Portfolio Management

Asset allocation, rebalancing and tracking performance over time.

MOD 12
Live Market Practice Sessions

Real trade walkthroughs, paper trading and guided live analysis.
